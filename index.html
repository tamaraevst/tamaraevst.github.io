I am a PhD student at Department of Aplpied Mathematics and Theoretical Physics at University of Cambrdige. I specialise in Numerical Realtivity and Tests of General Relativity using gravitational waves. I am part of an amazing numerical relativity code collaboration GRChombo. I am also a member of LIGO-Virgo-KAGRA collaboration, which made the first observation of the gravitational wave event GW150914 from a binary black merger.

My work concerns simualtions of binary systems mainly consisting of black holes and boson stars. Understading the dynamics in modified theories of gravity and th effects of "new matter", like scalar fields, have been the primary foci of my studies. The list of my publications can be found here.
